FAQ

What is NOCTA?

NOCTA is a sub-label of Nike created by Drake. The name is inspired by the night, referencing the creative hours when ideas come alive.

How do I find my size?

We recommend checking our size guide. Most items run true to size. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for a more relaxed fit.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business day delivery. Free shipping on orders over $100.

Are NOCTA products authentic?

Yes, all products are 100% authentic and sourced directly. Every item comes with original packaging and tags.

How can I contact you?

You can reach us anytime at contact@shopnocta.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
